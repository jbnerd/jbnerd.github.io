About Me

Who am I?

I'm a Junior at Birla Institute Technology and Science, Pilani pursuing Engineering in Computer Science. I am a keen learner and a careful observer in every field that I come across. I like to keep Exploring and Learning something new everyday. I believe that there is a lot to learn even from the smallest of things around me.

What are my Research Interests?

I have extensively worked in various sub-fields of Data Analytics including Natural Language Processing, Stream Mining and Mathematical Modelling. Recently, I have developed a strong interest in techniques addressing problems of Artificial Intelligence. I am typically interested in finding out how an Intelligent agent learns and so I have explored the use of Deep Learning in Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing and Reinforcement Learning. In the future I want to work and contribute towards the development of a Universal AI agent.

What is my thinking approach?

I am particularly interested in Logic based thinking and Problem Solving. Spotting Patterns and Relations comes to me very naturally as I have a knack of visualization. It helps me develop my knowledge in a greater depth. I am good at explaining various phenomena based on simple analogies for clarification of concepts. I have a habit of pondering over answers to a myriad of questions on human consciousness and existence. I often find myself astounded by the thinking styles of the great minds that have shaped the history and the science of today.
