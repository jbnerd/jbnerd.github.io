About Me

Who am I?

I'm a Junior student at Birla Institute Technology and Science, Pilani pursuing Engineering in Computer Science. I am a keen learner and a careful observer in every field that I come across. I like to keep Exploring and Learning something new everyday. I believe that there is a lot to learn even from the smallest of things around me.

What are my Research Interests?

I have some inclination towards Data Science and have worked on more than a couple of projects; involving Mining of continuous Data Streams, Model Building and some Intuitive Feature Generation. I am intrigued by various facets of Foundational Computer Science and Mathematics. I'm also greatly interested in Design and Analysis of Algorithms. In the future I want to contribute towards the development of Quantum Computing by pursuing research in that field.

What is my thinking approach?

I am particularly interested in Logic based thinking and Problem Solving. Spotting Patterns and Relations comes to me very naturally as I have a great knack of visualization. It helps me develop my knowledge in a greater depth. I am good at explaining various phenomena based on simple analogies for clarification of concepts. I have a habit of pondering over answers to a myriad of questions of human consciousness and existence. I often find myself astounded by the thinking styles of the great minds that have shaped the history and the science of today.

What I do in my free time

I have small talent for playing table tennis, self taught in free time. I also have a soft spot for Role Playing Computer Games since my childhood. Watching mind-intense movies and TV series is my favourite pastime. At other times I employ my programming skills to test by solving the Problems offered by Project Euler and Matasano's Cryptopals.
